I. The Prologue

Dearest Reader, we present the chronicles of Jessica and Nicolas. Their narrative is one woven of profound affection, steadfast friendship, and perhaps a touch of mathematical precision. It was a courtship both slow and gentle, commencing in the autumn of 2023.

II. Origins

Two souls from distant shores: Nicolas, hailing from the peaks of Colombia, and Jessica, a daughter of the sun-drenched South Florida. Though separated by vast geographies, they shared a common devotion to the grand tapestry of history.

III. The Encounter

Their meeting transpired amidst the vibrant spirit of Miami in September 2023. Little did they know the universe was arranging the scene for its greatest love story.

IV. Manhattan Adventures

The second rendezvous occurred in the grand metropolis of New York. A weekend of laughter was spent traversing their favorite city in the world.

V. The Hudson River

Destiny soon called them to Jersey City. As Jessica often explains to her father now: “It's on the other side of the Hudson River, Dad.”

VI. The Covenant

On the twenty-second of August, 2025, Nicolas requested Jessica’s hand in marriage. It was a moment of profound commitment.

VII. Jersey Life

They now dwell in the historic downtown of Jersey City, proud of their neighborhood and the charms of the "Sixth Borough."

VIII. The Eternal Bond

They prepare to dance within the grand halls of The Manor at Glen Cove. Sincerely, Jessica and Nicolas.
